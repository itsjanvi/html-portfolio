Superb animation turns Disneyâ€™s tale of a grumpy old man and plump little boy into a touching and exciting flight of fancy. A beautiful animated movie which will make you laugh and cry at the same time.

Zindagi Na Milegi Dobara

Story about three friends who meets to complete their pact and learns something new by overcoming their fears. Through the lives of three friends, presents the world of contemporary Indian youth, who are successful, affluent yuppies. In mood and execution the film is very modern, light, yet it entails complex relationships in family and between friends. The movie is non-sentimental and realistic, but it is emotional, and surprisingly enough, quite poetic.

The Princess Diaries

Based on the Meg Cabot book of the same name, this romantic family comedy follows the story of a 15 year old social outcast named Mia (Anne Hathaway). One day, she gets the chance to meet her grandmother for the first time who just happens to be the queen of the fiction country of Genovia. A year ago, Mia's father tragically passed away meaning she is now the true heir to the throne.Through this process, she has to learn to become a true princess. It is really enjoyable because the characters have really strong friendships which means they have lots of fun together.

Jab we met

One of the finest Romanctic love stories to come out of Indian Cinema. A simple story told in the most interesting and enjoyable manner. The film's first hour is enjoyable, the second is exactly the same. 'Jab We Met' holds your attention from almost start to end, leaving the viewer completely satisfied.
